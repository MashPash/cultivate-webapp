Gardening Information

Learn more about different categories of plants and how to care for them

Indoor Plants

Indoor plants are perfect for adding a touch of greenery to your home. They thrive in controlled indoor environments and can improve air quality. Popular indoor plants include:

  • Snake Plants
  • Spider Plants
  • Pothos

Outdoor Plants

Outdoor plants require more sunlight and space to grow. Here are some tips for successful outdoor gardening:

  1. Choose the right location with adequate sunlight.
  2. Prepare the soil with compost and organic matter.
  3. Water plants early in the morning.
